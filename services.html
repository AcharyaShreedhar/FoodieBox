Ordering food with FoodieBox is as easy as 1-2-3!

Step 1: Browse and choose a restaurant - with hundreds of popular restaurants to choose from, browse through our website or mobile app to find the restaurant that best suits your mood and taste buds.
Step 2: Select your food - once you’ve found your restaurant, select the dishes you want from their menu. The prices on our website are exactly the same as those on the restaurant’s menu.
Step 3: Confirm your order and delivery details - after selecting your food, provide us with your delivery details, including your detailed address, date and time for delivery, and any other special instructions.
You can pay in cash upon delivery. Alternatively, you can also place your order over the phone during our delivery hours at 5197749270
