Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1) How can I place an order on the website?
    Customers can browse the menu, select items, and add them to the cart. They can then proceed to checkout, provide delivery or pickup details, and pay for the order.
  • 2) What is the delivery time for my order?
    The delivery time may vary depending on the restaurant's location, the distance to the customer, and the volume of orders. The estimated delivery time is usually provided during the checkout process.
  • 3) How do I track my order once it is placed?
    You can track your order through a website or mobile app. Customers may receive notifications or updates on the status of their order, such as preparation, dispatch, or delivery.
